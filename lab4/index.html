  3. Start rinsing your hair with cold - Medium cold temperature water
  4. First shampoo

  5. Rinse shampop
  6. Second Shampoo
    • Grab a smaller amount of shampoo and wash the ends of your hair
  7. Rinse shampoo
  8. Grab your conditioner and add appropriate amount for your depending on hair length and texture
  9. Rinse conditioner off
  10. Continue with shower routine
  11. Once, you have finished use microfiber towel/ cotton shirt to dry your hair
  12. Then apply a coin sized amount of leave in conditioner
  13. Apply all over your hair, and scrunch
  14. Repeat process but with soft gel.
  15. Let hair air dry for 15 minutes
  16. Then apply a diffuser on your hairdryer
  17. Difusse until your is hair is dry
  18. Fluff out your hair
  19. Apply light oil to break the gel cast
