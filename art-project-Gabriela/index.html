This is Me!

Hi everyone! My name is Gabriela and welcome to my webpage! Here you will learn about different organizations and or clubs in Santa Cruz that support the LGTBQ+ community. If this sounds interesting, then stick around and EXPLORE!

Helpful Resources for the LGBTQ+ Community:

UCSC Lionel Cantú Queer Center:

The Lionel Cantú Queer Resource Center envisions a campus that unites, keeps, and supports queer and trans students, employees, professors, and alumni. They picture gay and trans individuals thriving in inclusive communities where they are fully accepted, cherished, and celebrated. They also provide even more resources for people of color and organizations that people can join.

Queer Youth Task Force of Santa Cruz County:

By developing and implementing new resources and enhancing current ones, QYTF works to enhance the quality of life for all LGBTIQ kids in our community as well as children reared in homes where LGBTIQ people reside. Increasing knowledge of community resources, encouraging service collaboration, and much more!

Supporting and Caring for our Latinx LGBTQ+ Youth:

This compaign's aim is to ensure that all LGBTQ+ people are regarded as complete and equal citizens within our movement, across our nation, and globally, with a focus on those of us who are trans, people of color, and HIV+.
